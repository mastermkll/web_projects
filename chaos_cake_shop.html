📖 Our Story

A Bakery Born from Chaos & Love

👨‍🍳

Chaos Cake Shop wasn't planned — it happened the way great things do. What started as a sourdough obsession in a tiny Malad kitchen, feeding neighbours with loaves wrapped in brown paper, became something bigger than we ever imagined.

Our founder, Arjun Kapoor, spent years perfecting his starter culture, named "Dadi" after his grandmother who first taught him the patience that baking demands. The philosophy was simple: use the best ingredients you can find, take the time you need, and never cut corners.

Today, Chaos Cake Shop is Malad's beloved neighbourhood bakery — a place where sourdough is still made the old way, pastries are laminated by hand, and your coffee is brewed with intention.

🕐 Our Journey

The Timeline

2019
The First Loaf
Arjun bakes his first proper sourdough. Gives it to neighbours. They ask for more.
2020
Lockdown Baking
Home deliveries begin during lockdown. Word spreads through WhatsApp groups across Malad.
2021
The Little Counter
We open a tiny counter in Malad West. 12 items. Sold out by 10am every day.
2022
The Café is Born
We move to a bigger space. Add an espresso machine, a few tables, and a whole lot of plants.
2024
Growing Together
500+ daily customers, new pastry menu, and still the same starter "Dadi" from day one.
🌿 Our Philosophy

What We Believe In

🌾

Honest Ingredients

We use unbleached flour, local dairy, seasonal produce and nothing artificial. If we can't pronounce it, it doesn't go in your bread.

⏳

Slow is Good

Our sourdough ferments for 24–36 hours. You can't rush flavour. Patience is our most important ingredient.

🏘️

Community First

Chaos Cake Shop is your bakery. We want you to feel at home here, whether you're grabbing a loaf or spending a whole morning with your laptop.
